Volunteering in Marin County

Cities/area within Marin County:
Belvedere, Corte Madera, Fairfax, Larkspur, Mill Valley, Novato, Ross, San Anselmo, San Rafael, Sausalito, Tiburon

Ross:
Volunteer at Cedars which aims to offer a high quality of life to individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities in order to foster independence, defend dignity, and realization of each person’s full potential. Various positions available. If you're interested, click here.

The Red Cross:
If you're looking to help donate blood then sign up for your local Red Cross Club in the Marin County. You can find more information here.

Halleck Creek Ranch Park:
Volunteer at Halleck Creek Ranch where children and adults with disabilities engage with dedicated volunteers and gentle horses to enjoy a day of therapy and fun. No horse experience necessary. Click here for more details.

Marin County Public Parks:
If you have a lot of time on your hands then you can help clean the various Marin County Parks. More information and sign-ups, click here.

San Rafael Rescue Dogs:
Help special-needs participants bathe and walk dogs or maintain the facility at Jessica’s Haven and Rescue. Click here for more information.

Mountain Tam State park:
As a volunteer here, you would become a hike docent for the park and encourage others to become more connected to nature. If you see yourself doing this, click here.

San Rafael Library:
Volunteer with Friends Books to help sort and move books to storage units. If this appeals to you, click here for more information.

Novato Community of Christ and Food Pantry:
Help set up, unload and organize food, and clean up at this food pantry. If you're interested, click here.

Slide Ranch:
Volunteer under the supervision of the trained staff at Slide Ranch. Volunteers contribute to Slide Ranch in a variety of ways, including working in our organic garden (weeding, pruning, preparing garden beds, planting and pest control), composting, restoring native plants and removing invasive plants, and maintaining trails and beach access. For more details, click here.

Novato South Little League Baseball:
Here, you will help maintain and prepare the baseball fields for the upcoming baseball season. For more information, click here.

Milli Valley; SaveABunny:
SaveABunny is a nonprofit group dedicated to rescuing domestic rabbits facing euthanasia at shelters and educating people about the joys of living with a house rabbit. To learn more about this organization, click here.
