Daly City Libraries:
At all Daly City libraries, teens can work 2-4 hour shifts and assist with various programs at the library. To volunteer at a library in Daly City, click here.

The North Peninisula Food Pantry and Dining Hall of Daly City:
On Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays, volunteers here can either help sort and pack donated food for distribution in the food pantry or serve food in the dining hall. For more information, click here.
