Volunteering in San Francisco

Cities within San Francisco County:
San Francisco

Family Dog Rescue (animal shelter):
The training you recieve while volunteering teaches you the process of rescuing dogs and safely finding a home for them, how the organizations are run, animal behavior, shelter cleanliness, and how to properly walk and socialize the dogs for their routine. Click here for more information.

Adopt a family reception:
Fill out some paperwork and determine what kind of family you would like to adopt and then you will receive a need and want wish list for each person. Click here for more info and sign-ups.

Assistant Hospitality Monitors at the sanctuary of Saint John the Evangelist:
Here, you will help and out food and essential supplies to the homeless. Click here for more information.
